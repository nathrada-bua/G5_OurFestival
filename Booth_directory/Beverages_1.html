About

Yen Yen Bamboo was born from a love of Thai herbal traditions and the refreshing simplicity of nature. Inspired by the cooling phrase “yen yen” and the rustic charm of bamboo, the stall was designed for festivals and outdoor gatherings. It offers four signature drinks—Pandan Cooler, Lemongrass Breeze, Longan Sweet Tea, and Fresh Coconut Water—each crafted to soothe and hydrate under the tropical sun. Using local ingredients and timeless recipes, Yen Yen Bamboo blends tradition with modern flair, inviting everyone to pause, sip, and enjoy the gentle breeze of Thai refreshment.
